About Kobeshi

All You Can Eat Steak and Shabu Shabu

A 5-star hotel ambience that you can find at Street Gallery Level 2, Pondok Indah Mall. A different yet extraordinary concept from a restaurant that served All You Can Eat STEAK, Shabu-Shabu, Japanese Buffet, Sushi, Dessert, and Beverages all in one package with 144 seating capacity.

Office meetings, family gathering, birthday celebrations, or any occasions that you want to spend the time with us, we got you covered! Kobeshi VIP room designated to make you and your relatives feel special.
